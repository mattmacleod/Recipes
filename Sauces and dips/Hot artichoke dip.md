# Hot Artichoke Dip

> This recipe sounds absolutely disgusting but it might be the single most delicious thing ever created. I think I got the recipe from Hellman'sâ€¦

## Attributes

- Servings: 4-5
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cooking time: 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1x400g can of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 250ml mayonnaise
- 100g grated parmesan cheese
- 50g grated cheddar cheese

## Method

1. Pre-heat oven to 180C/fan 160C

2. Combine all ingredients in a medium casserole dish, then bake uncovered for about 30 minutes until golden and bubbly. Serve with bread.
